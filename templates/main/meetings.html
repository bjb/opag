The Ottawa Python Author's Group meetings are on the second Tuesday of every month from 19:00 - 21:00. The location is currently 3444 ME at Carleton U.

Here is a map of Carleton University. 3444 ME is on level 3 (one above ground) in block 4 of Mackenzie Engineering - Building 10 on the map. Its the block closest to Architecture (Building 22). On the map, its the block just to the left of the number '10' on the map. Very close to the O-train station, if you come that way. Bus stops almost immediately out front. Parking Lot 2 is the closest, but parking is always a problem in the centre of campus at prime time. Here is a link to a Google map of Carleton U., in case you need driving directions.
