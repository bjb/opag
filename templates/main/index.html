elcome to the home of the Ottawa Python Authors Group!

This is a group devoted to learning, using and providing resources for the programming language Python (click on the Python image on the top-left of this page for the Python Homepage). OPAG also serves as a general gathering place for Python programmers from the Ottawa region and beyond.

We maintain an active mailing list and an IRC channel (irc.oftc.net, #opag), for our discussions, and we meet once a month, typically at Carleton University, but we've been known to venture out into the real world from time to time as well.
